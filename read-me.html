READ ME

Forgotten corners. Unseen angles. Stories framed to make you look again. At Snafu Pictures, our mission is to see things differently, to push past the noise of current events and explore the crumbling edges. Our subjects range from hurricanes to hedge funds, but always with a focus on the human strangeness in their midst. In a media landscape littered with easy simplifications, we opt to make complexity compelling again.

How we tell our stories is as important as the stories we tell. Capture the difference.
